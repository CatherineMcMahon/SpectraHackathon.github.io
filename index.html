Exploring the Tech Space Together

Spectra is a brand-new all-ladies hackathon coming to the West Coast! Join us the weekend of July 22nd to July 24th for an awesome event with 200 fellow women in technology.

This is a unique event bringing together female college students and professionals from all around the world. Free from the gender stereotypes that can often taint the male-dominated tech industry, Spectra offers hackers a safe and inviting environment for women to experiment and work comfortably with technology at any experience level.

Every woman should have the opportunity to gain confidence in her technical abilities and be motivated to make her own impact in computer science. So what are you waiting for? Come learn new skills, inspire one another, and build long-lasting connections at Spectra.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

You’ve got questions, we’ve got answers.

What is a hackathon?

In this case, the word "hack" describes how multiple technologies can be used together in new and creative ways. Teams will spend the weekend creating a website, a web app, a mobile app, software, or hardware creations. The Spectra team believes that the most important aspect of a hackathon is the community it generates and the skills that attendees can take away with them at the end of the event.

Who can attend?

Anyone who identifies as female can register to hack! If you don’t identify as female but are supportive of women in tech, we would love your help as a volunteer.

What is the cost to attend?

Attendance is 100% free. That includes Wi-Fi access, tables, chairs, food, drinks, hella swag, mentors, an amazing opportunity to learn and network, and more!

How do I register?

Registration isn't open yet, but sign up for our mailing list to find out when it is!

What if I don’t know how to code?

You’ve found the perfect place and time to learn! There will be dedicated workshops and mentors to ease your way through your Spectra experience. And don't worry, applications are not filtered by technical background. Our focus is on making Spectra an awesome event for everyone involved, which means we will welcome attendees at any level of expertise. After all, everyone had to start somewhere!

What if I don’t have a team or an idea yet?

Totally fine - if you don’t have a team now, don’t stress! We’ll have team formation and ideation events geared towards helping you find people to work with.

What do I need to bring?

All you'll need is a valid photo ID (a student ID works), and your laptop, and charger. Also, a pillow and some toiletries never hurt anyone!

What if my question wasn't answered here?

Shoot us an email at spectradirectors@gmail.com and we'll help you with anything you need!
